Plastic Pollution in the Great Lakes

(December 19, 2016) RIT's press release for my paper (with Eric Hittigner at RIT) that produced the first estimates of plastic pollution entering into and floating in the Great Lakes. We estimate that around 10,000 metric tons of plastic enter the Great Lakes each year and several tons of small plastic particles are floating in the Lakes. A preprint of the paper can be found here and video of our simulations can be found under the Projcets/Data tab.
